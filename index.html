Frequently Asked Question

Event design is the process of creating a vision for an event by crafting a blueprint of all the visual details around said occasion to transform your space, while event planning is working out all the logistics of your event.On the other hand event planning is the process of managing a project such as a meeting, convention, tradeshow, ceremony, team building activity, party, or convention.

Planning an event can take from weeks to months, it really depends on the size and the details that the event involves. Having enough time allows you to comfortably cover different areas and prepare yourself for potential scenarios that might happen so you can consider various options prior the date. You always need more time than you think!

We request that you cancel at least 48 hours before a scheduled event. This gives us the opportunity to fill the event. You may cancel by phone or online here. If you have to cancel your event, we offer you a credit to your account if you cancel before the 48 hours, but do not offer refunds. You may use these credits towards any future event. However, if you do not cancel prior to the 48 hours, you will lose the payment for the event. The owner has the only right to be flexible here.

Your event budget should be decided from the very beginning of the event planning stage. Once your checklist is created, the budget should be created around it. Keep track of your expenses and document when each payment and the method it was paid. One little slip up, or add-on to the event budget can make or break the entire outcome of the event, so monitor throughout, and stay organised.
